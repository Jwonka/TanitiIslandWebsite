Restaurants

Restaurant hours are 8 am to 9 pm daily.

    Taniti currently has 10 restaurants:
  • Five serve mostly local fish and rice.
  • Three serve American-style meals.
  • Two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store open 24 hours a day. The supermarkets and grocery stores are open 6 am until 8 pm.
